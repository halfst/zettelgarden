# Frontmatter

## Summary
Frontmatter is the section at the top of a markdown document that stores document metadata in YAML key-value pairs. It starts and ends with three dashes like so:

```
    --- 
    tags: [tag1, tag2, tag3]
    title: My Document's Title
    date: 2023-01-23
    ---
```
